2. What We Collect

We only collect what we really need to make Swidheat better for you:

  • Name and email (if you subscribe to our newsletter or submit forms).
  • Technical info (IP address, browser/device info, interactions) for improving the website experience.

3. Newsletter Data

Subscribe to Swidheat updates and we’ll store your name and email in Google Sheets (connected to our Gmail account). This is solely for sending you news, product info, and updates.

Unsubscribe anytime via the link in the emails or our website. Your data will then be removed from our list.

4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site. Nothing happens until you click “Accept” on the cookie banner. IP anonymization is enabled, so your full IP is never stored.

5. Cookies

We use cookies only for:

  • Analytics – to measure how people interact with the site (after consent)
  • Remembering your cookie preferences

6. Third-Party Services

Some tools help us run Swidtech:

  • Google – Analytics, Gmail, Sheets
  • Tally – newsletter & form handling
  • Cloudflare – email routing & security
  • GitHub – website hosting

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your newsletter info as long as you’re subscribed. Analytics data is processed according to Google Analytics retention rules. Once you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our records.

8. Legal Basis

Your data is processed because:

  • You gave consent – subscribing or accepting cookies.
  • Legitimate interest – to operate the website safely and efficiently.

9. Your GDPR Rights

You can always:

  • Access your data
  • Correct it
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Contact Molnár Donát Levente to exercise any of these rights.

10. Children

We protect your data with reasonable measures, including using Cloudflare services to help secure our website and prevent attacks. However, no system on the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
